2. System Requirements

CeeGee requires the following equipment:

   - 100% IBM compatible computer with an 80486 or Pentium processor
   - VGA graphics card with at least 256KB of video memory
   - VGA monitor
   - Hard disk drive with at least 3MB of available space
   - 640KB of available RAM

Optional components:

   - Sound Blaster or compatible sound card for digitized sound effects and
     full musical soundtrack
   - AdLib or compatible music card for full musical soundtrack
   - Connected mouse with drivers installed

Please contact us if the game doesn't work properly despite all
necessary components being present.
